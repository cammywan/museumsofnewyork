Here are a couple reccomendations for travelers in New York,
from Cammy and Vukan.

Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum

Famous geometric shape architecture with wide-ranging, fantastic art selection.

Metropolitan Museum of Art (MET)

The lardest art museum in the United States

The Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)

Forever at the forefront, the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) is not only devoted to presenting the best in contemporary art, but also to promoting the understanding of modern art and expanding the definition of what is considered art in the first place.

American Museum of Natural History (AMNH)

The American Museum of Natural History is one of the largest and most innovative natural history museums in the world.
