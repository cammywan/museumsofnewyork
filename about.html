About

This is a website aimed to introduce some great museums located in New York City.

It gives a detailed information about the four famous and universally-acknowledged museums - Guggenheim, Metropolitan Museum of Art, Museum of Modern Art and American Museum of Natural History.

It has a brief introduction or personal experience of the museum. It also includes the information about admission, hours, directions etc.

Some brief reviews of the museum would also give you an idea of what the museum is like and what kind of experience the museum offers.

This is a website designed by Cammy Wan, a college student at Borough of Manhattan Community College.
