Metropolitan Museum of Art

The lardest art museum in the United States
Content by Vukan and Cammy Wan

This is the Metropolitan Museum of Art located in upper east side on the corner of 82 street and 5th ave.

The first time i went there was when i came to New York 2009.

This place is meaningful to me as a young boy moving to a big city where i didn't know anybody this museum because my safe heaven, my go to place to make friends to learn and to feel the beauty of the city.

People should visit it because the museum contains so much history in it that we as people need to grow to appreciate, plus if one has nothing to do a museum is a fun place to go to because you can be there all day and not feel it.

The only thing people should know to go there is to be ready to spend the entire day there, to come out with some knowledge they didn't have before. The best way to get there is by the 6 train and the walking another 15 minutes.

ADDRESS:

1000 5th Ave, New York, NY 10028

WEBSITE:

www.metmuseum.org

DIRECTIONS:

Travel to the museum on the 4 5 6 and Q subway lines, or the M1, M2, M3, or M4 bus lines on Madison or Fifth Avenue.

HOURS:

Sunday–Thursday: 10 am–5:30 pm

Friday and Saturday: 10 am–9 pm

Open Seven Days a Week

Expect Holiday Exceptions

SUGGESTED ADMISSION:

Adults $25

Students $12

Seniors (65+) $17

Children (below 12) Free

Members Free

Exhibitions are free with Museum admission.
